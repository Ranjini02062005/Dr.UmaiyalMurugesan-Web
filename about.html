Who We Are

The Story of Umaiyal

Dr. Umaiyal Murugesan is a leader whose journey has been shaped by a deep sense of purpose and commitment to social change. From grassroots engagement to broader community leadership, every step reflects a dedication to empowering those around her.

Nari Sakthi Umaiyal is a platform built on the belief that true progress begins with empowered individuals — especially women. Every initiative is guided by values of integrity, inclusiveness, and service.

Her leadership model is built on listening, collaborating, and creating sustainable change. She believes in the power of education, awareness, and community as the pillars of a stronger society.

Our Purpose

Vision & Mission

Our Vision

To build a society where women are empowered, confident, and actively contributing to meaningful progress. A society that values every individual's potential and provides equal opportunity for growth and leadership.

Our Mission

To promote awareness, create opportunities, and support initiatives that strengthen communities and improve quality of life. To be a catalyst for positive social change through service, education, and community engagement.
